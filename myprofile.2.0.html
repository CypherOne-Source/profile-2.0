What is it?

 Project Athena. The basics for Athena (working title) is simple. Most people avoid it, some simply ignore it. Cyber security tends to bore people. Non-Tech savvy people wouldn’t for the most part have any idea until it's too late. The principle is simple, Offer a Software as a Service. Offer something different and give people better education when it comes to understanding the perils of being online. I can name a handful of family and friends who thinks it’s a simple as having an Anti-Virus program installed and that’s all that’s needed. I honestly believe that there needs to be better education on what Cyber Security is all about. What motivates me? Why am I so passionate about educating everyone? It’s simple I have family, friends and even my children who are social media active, who use tik-tok every day and have no idea of the risk that are lurking out there. I want to be able to offer a range of services, an app, web-based education. Is it a lot to take on? Sure, it is, because every day there's new risk. But I believe that just one person can make a difference. What will I be offering, what exactly will it do? If you look at the consensus of the likes of AVG, Mcafee anti-virus or Malware software people get bombarded by it in Google searches, email spam and even the likes of Facebook with targeted ads, sponsored advertising. This project or SaaS is more than that, It comes down to grassroots level service. People sign up, that get a better education when it comes to having an online presence, that get access to our services wherever they are. Bottom line is, it’s for the people run by me. 

With recent events, more and more cyber-attacks and what can only be something that’s going to get worse. Athena (WIP) will be simple and effective and not some big corporation that people are afraid to deal with, afraid to call tech support. They’ll have an app to access be it iOS or Android, it will have web-based access, people can ask for help, ask for better cyber security education. People can say, “Hey I don’t understand this, can you help? “I want to be said yes I can. I want to all the nana’s, they grandparents who aren’t tech savvy. That’s what I want to offer. Onwards, what’s needed? Or how do I get there and what sort of problems will I encounter? I think that the How or what sections are simple. With the likes of Apples app development program and even with MIT App Inventor make this a lot easier. I have already signed up with Apples iOS developer program and started poking around with MIT app maker. Of course, being certified and properly educated with the right skill set is a huge plus. The web-based side of things will need to be scalable, be able to handle large numbers, a solid framework. It will obviously need a service like Cloudflare's DDos protection services, a reliable and safe web host/domain services and decent backup servers. Skill that I’d need to be able to get this up and running, being certified in CISM, Cyber Security protocols, a good understanding of pentesting, Android/iOS certified. Be able to maintain cloud base tech, Have the skills to be able to maintain web hosting and domain name security.
